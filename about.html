Metro Manila Bus Corporation (MMBC) delivers safe, reliable and comfortable travel services for the Filipino community.

MMBC connects Metro Manila to Ayala, Laguna and several destinations, with 24/7 service for passengers and freight.

Our new digital booking lets you get moving in just a few clicks: choose your route, select your schedule, and pay securely — speedy, easy, hassle free!

MMBC Online Booking Process

  1. Visit MMBC Booking: Go to our online platform.
  2. Choose Trip Details: Pick your destination, date, and time.
  3. Payment: Enter details and select a payment method.
  4. Confirmation: Receive your ticket via email, and exchange at the terminal for boarding.
